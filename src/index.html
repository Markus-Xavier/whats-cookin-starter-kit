Spaghetti & Meatballs

tags: #meat #main #italian #sauce

Ingredients Cost $10.00

Ingredients

  • Cheese 0.5 cups
  • Pasta 1 cup
  • Ground beef 1 cup
  • Tomato sauce 2 cups

Instructions

  1. Cook the beef with the garlic
  2. Blend the tomatoes and cook them until reduced
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. Mix with the cooked pasta
